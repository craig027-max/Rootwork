Tiny uppercase mono label for tiers, card counts, status, and star totals.

```jsx
<Badge jewel="cobalt">Tier 3 · Scholar</Badge>
<Badge variant="solid" jewel="jade">New</Badge>
<Badge variant="gold" icon="★">5 Stars</Badge>
```

Variants: `outline` (jewel hairline, default), `solid` (jewel gradient fill), `gold` (star/premium fill). Always UPPERCASE JetBrains Mono — the casing is the type. One emoji icon max.
